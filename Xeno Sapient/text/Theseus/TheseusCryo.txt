The CRYOGENICS room of the Theseus. This is the room that personal are stored  during the long flights to and from the work location in deep space. During this travel time, the entire floorpan is taken up by rows of these cryo pods. 5 rows of 4 pods deep, with another parallel grid of 20 pods hanging from the ceiling just above. During construction, this part of the ship is transformed into a crew sleeping quarters, with the cryo pods doubling as beds. 

There is a door-lock to the MAIN ROOM. (West)
